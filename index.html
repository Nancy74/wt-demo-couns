How I can help

Whatever you're carrying,
there is a way through.

I work with adults experiencing a wide range of emotional and psychological difficulties. Here are some of the areas I specialise in.

🌿

Anxiety & Stress

Persistent worry, panic, overwhelm or burnout. Together we'll understand your anxiety and find calmer, steadier ground.

🌙

Depression & Low Mood

When life feels flat, heavy or meaningless — therapy can help you reconnect with a sense of self, purpose and possibility.

🕊

Grief & Loss

Bereavement, miscarriage, relationship endings or any kind of loss. I offer an unhurried space to sit with what you're feeling.

🤝

Relationships

Difficult family patterns, friendship struggles, romantic challenges — exploring how you relate to yourself and others.

🔄

Life Transitions

Career change, parenthood, divorce, identity shifts. Therapy helps you navigate change with clarity and confidence.

🌱

Trauma & Identity

Paced, safe work with difficult memories, PTSD, self-esteem, and questions of belonging — especially for those navigating culture and heritage.

About Diana

Therapy rooted in
warmth and lived experience.

I'm Diana Calloway, an integrative counsellor and BACP registered psychotherapist. My practice draws on person-centred, psychodynamic and CBT approaches — adapting to what you need most.

I have a particular warmth for supporting Black, mixed-heritage and minority ethnic clients navigating identity, belonging and intergenerational experiences.

I believe deeply in the therapeutic relationship — that real healing begins when you feel genuinely seen.
